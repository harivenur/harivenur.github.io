Acknowledgement

I acknowledge these external factors that has provided me with tremendous privileges and advantages throughout my life:

(1) My Savarna-Hindu identity has historically given me a huge and unfair socio-economic advantage over Avarnas (Dalits and Adivasis). I condemn all past and continuing atrocities, including implicit and explicit discrimination and alienation, by Savarna Hindus and the political-right against Avarnas in and outside the Indian subcontinent.

(2) Kerala's unique socio-political and cultural advancements, stemming from prevalent egalitarian and socialist ideologies in the region, have provided me with a significant edge over people from other Indian states throughout my life and career. I condemn all right-wing efforts to ignore, denigrate, ridicule, and renounce Kerala's egalitarian advancements.

(3) For many years, a socialist and secular India that evolved from the joint ethos of Gandhian-Nehruvian-Ambedkarite-Leftist pluralism gave me peace, dignity, equality, and freedom of expression, and bestowed upon me ridiculously cheap education and medical care. I am eternally grateful to the visionaries that made it possible. I condemn all right-wing efforts that strive to make India a narrow, unilingual, unipolar, theocratic oligarchy.

(4) The unbelievable selflessness and integrity of my parents, who invested all their money, time, and health in caring for and educating their children, helped me come this far. I do not take their sacrifices for granted.
